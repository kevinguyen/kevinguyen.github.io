We Are Rising Phoenix

We are a non profit organization that has been around for over a decade practicing the art of modern Lion Dancing in San Jose, California. Consisted of high school students and young professionals, we strive to keep the traditional art of lion dance alive. We are a young and dynamic team who's aim is to impress.
